\begin{abstract}

Sazetak se pise na kraju. Obicno ne sadrzi preko 150 rijeci, a ukratko opisuje motivaciju vaseg rada (INTRODUCTION), sto ste htjeli napraviti (OBJECTIVE), kako ste to napravili (METHODS) i sto ste postigli (RESULTS)

\textit{The abstract is written at the end. It usually does not exceed 150 words and briefly describes the motivation of your work (INTRODUCTION), what you intended to do (OBJECTIVE), how you did it (METHODS), and what you achieved (RESULTS).}

\end{abstract}


%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
\section{\textbf{INTRODUCTION}}

U uvodu treba kratko opisati problem kojim se bavite, koji vam je bio cilj, kako ste to mislili ostvariti i sto ste u konacnici postigli. U ovom poglavlju svakako referencirate relevantne radove, a svakako pogledajte kako je to napravljeno u radu koji ste imali za seminar.

\textit{In the introduction, you should briefly describe the problem you are addressing, what your goal was, how you intended to achieve it, and what you ultimately accomplished. In this chapter, be sure to reference relevant papers, and definitely take a look at how this was done in the paper you had for your seminar.}


\section{\textbf{METHODOLOGY}}

Metodologija opsirnije opisuje kako ste odlucili ostvariti vaЕЎ cilj, odnosno kako ste osmislili algoritam i sl. Savjetujem skicirati tok podataka u vaЕЎem algoritmu / konfiguraciju algoritma ili sl.

\textit{The methodology describes in more detail how you decided to achieve your goal, that is, how you designed the algorithm, etc. Sketching the data flow in your algorithm, the algorithm configuration, or similar is highly recommended.}


\section{\textbf{CASE STUDY}}

Ovdje opisujete koje ste podatke koristili, kako ste osmislili svoj eksperiment, odnosno studiju slucaja (eng. \textit{case study}), sto ona podrazumijeva, kako ste podijelili podatke, kako ste vrednovali rezultate,... Moze se podijeliti u nekoliko pod poglavlja kao npr.:

    \subsection{Datasets}
    opisati kompoziciju podataka, koji podaci su vam bili za ucenje, koji za testiranje...

    \subsection{Evaluation}
    koje mjere koristite za vrednovanje modela i dobivenih rezultata.



\textit{Here you describe which data you used, how you designed your experiment, that is, the case study (eng. case study), what it entails, how you split the data, how you evaluated the results, etc. It can be divided into several subsections, such as:}

    \subsection{Datasets}
    \textit{describe the composition of the data, which data were used for training and which for testing...}
    
    \subsection{Evaluation}
    \textit{which metrics you use to evaluate the model and the obtained results.}
    


\section{\textbf{RESULTS and DISCUSSION}}

Ovdje navodite najvaЕѕnije rezultate koje ste ostvarili i opisujete kako ste ih prikazali (sto je prikazano na slikama i tablicama, kako citamo te slike i tablice te sto iz njih vidimo). Obavezno vidjeti kako je ovo poglavlje napravljeno u radu iz vaseg seminara. 
U diskusiji je potrebno detalnjije diskutirati rezultate - postoje li neki interesantni trendovi, koji su vasi zakljucci, postoje li neki izuzeci u vasim zakljuccima, koje su vase pretpostavke uzrocno-posljedicnih veza i sl.

\textit{Here you present the most important results you have achieved and describe how you have displayed them (what is shown in the figures and tables, how to read those figures and tables, and what can be observed from them). Be sure to see how this chapter was written in the paper from your seminar.
Here you may discuss the results in more detail вЂ” whether there are any interesting trends, what your conclusions are, whether there are any exceptions to your conclusions, what your assumptions about cause-and-effect relationships are, and so on.}


\section{\textbf{Conclusion}}

Ovdje jos jednom sumirate sto je bio cilj rada, koji su rezultati i jeste li ostvarili zadane ciljeve te koji bi mogli biti iduci koraci.

\textit{Here you once again summarize the objective of the work, the results obtained, whether you achieved the set goals, and what the next steps could be.}
